Leading the Way in Environmental Management

Cross Country Environmental Ltd provides expert environmental consultancy services to the energy infrastructure sector. We specialise in high-pressure gas pipelines, electricity cables, tunnels, and overhead powerlines.

Our Services

Environmental Management Plans

Comprehensive EMPs tailored for infrastructure projects, ensuring compliance with environmental regulations and best practices.

Ecological Surveys

Habitat assessments, protected species surveys, and ecological reporting to support planning and construction.

Environmental Permitting & Consents

Support with obtaining necessary permits and consents for projects, including liaison with regulatory bodies.

Construction Environmental Supervision

On-site environmental monitoring and support during construction to manage risk and ensure compliance.
